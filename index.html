About Me


Hello there, I'm Gemma, and I'm here to declare proudly that I am an unapologetic, over-educated cat enthusiast. If you happen to stumble upon my GitHub profile, you'll discover my digital alter ego: @smolTypo – a playful nod to my penchant for making those small errors that oddly haven't hindered my overall progress.


By profession, I am a pharmacist, by choice, a coding enthusiast, and by passion, a writer fueled by relentless curiosity. This introduction serves as the gateway to my GitHub repositories page, a canvas where I seamlessly blend together a tapestry of diverse interests and experiences, effortlessly merging the realms of science and creativity.


In the realm of pharmaceuticals, I have dedicated myself to the art of healing and caring for those in need. My mission extends beyond the walls of my pharmacy to positively impact the well-being of individuals. Here, in the world of coding, I dive deep into intricate problem-solving, igniting my fascination for unraveling complex challenges and fostering innovation.


Within the cozy confines of my home, I share my space with three delightful feline companions, each with their own distinct personality and quirks. They bring warmth to my daily life, a delightful contrast to my digital pursuits.


The call of adventure resonates in my heart, beckoning me towards the promise of new discoveries and fresh perspectives. I've embarked on countless journeys, immersing myself in diverse cultures, breathtaking landscapes, and the vibrant tapestry of human existence.


While I may not tread the path of particle physics in my professional life, I hold a profound admiration for the grandeur of space and the wonders of science. The cosmos, with its infinite mysteries, continues to captivate my imagination. It is within this realm of cosmic wonder that I find inspiration, nurturing my quest to explore the depths of our universe.


So, I invite you to join me on a journey through this coding project website, where science and art gracefully intertwine, and where curiosity acts as my guiding light. Together, let's delve into the intricate beauty of our world, celebrating the boundless possibilities that life has to offer.
